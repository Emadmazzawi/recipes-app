## Full Description (Max 4000 Characters)
Welcome to **Smart Recipes**, your ultimate culinary companion designed to make cooking smarter, faster, and more enjoyable! Whether you're a seasoned chef or a beginner looking to explore the kitchen, Smart Recipes brings the world of cooking directly to your smartphone with a beautiful, modern "Cool Blue" interface and powerful AI technology.

### 🌟 Key Features:

**🤖 AI-Powered Recipe Generation**
Stuck wondering what to make with the ingredients in your fridge? Just tell our intelligent AI culinary assistant what you have, and it will instantly generate a delicious, step-by-step recipe tailored just for you. No more food waste, just great meals!

**📚 Organize Your Culinary World**
Save your favorite recipes, create custom categories, and keep your cooking life organized. Our beautiful, glassmorphism-inspired UI ensures that managing your recipes is as visually pleasing as the meals you create.

**🌍 Multi-Language Support**
Cooking is universal, and so is our app. Smart Recipes offers full translation and Right-To-Left (RTL) support. Choose between English, Hebrew, and Arabic right from your personalized settings screen.

**🛒 Smart Shopping Lists**
Easily turn your recipe ingredients into practical, categorized shopping lists. Never forget a crucial ingredient at the grocery store again!

**🔄 Seamless Syncing & Security**
Your data is securely backed up and synced in the cloud. Log in safely using your Email or seamlessly via Google OAuth. Rest easy knowing your recipes are safe and accessible across your devices.

**📤 Native Web Sharing**
Found a recipe you love? Share it instantly with friends and family using your device's native sharing tools.
